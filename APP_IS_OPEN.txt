📱 The GUI window should be visible now!

If you don't see it:
- Check your taskbar for "traffic_control_app.py"
- Look for a dark blue window titled "Adaptive Traffic Signal Control System"

================================================================================
📖 HOW TO USE:
================================================================================

1. UPLOAD VIDEOS (top of window)
   - Click "📁 Upload Video" for Lane 1
   - Select a video file (e.g., videos/1.mp4)
   - Repeat for Lanes 2, 3, 4

2. PROCESS ALL VIDEOS
   - Click "🚀 Process All Videos"
   - Wait for "✓ All lanes processed!"
   - Vehicle counts will appear

3. CALCULATE TIMING
   - Click "⚡ Calculate Timing"
   - View results in the text box
   - Simulation window will open!

================================================================================
🎯 WHAT TO EXPECT:
================================================================================

The app will:
✓ Open your videos
✓ Detect vehicles (cars, buses, trucks, bikes, motorcycles)
✓ Count them per lane
✓ Calculate optimal traffic light timing
✓ Show live traffic light switching
